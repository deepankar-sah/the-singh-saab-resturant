## Story Body

**Section 1 — The Vision:**
```
Heading: Built From the Ground Up, On Purpose

Copy:
The Singh Saab began with a simple idea: dining out shouldn't just be 
about food. It should be an experience — one where every texture, every 
corner, every detail has been chosen with intent.

From the moment construction began, every decision — the flooring, 
the lighting, the seating — was made to create a space that feels 
warm, premium, and personal. Not a restaurant that looks like every 
other restaurant. A space with soul.
```

**Section 2 — The Name:**
```
Heading: What's in a Name

Copy:
"Singh Saab" isn't just a name — it's a feeling. In our part of the world, 
calling someone "Saab" is a mark of respect, warmth, and welcome. 

That's exactly the energy we wanted every guest to feel the moment they 
walk through our doors. Not just served — welcomed, like an honoured guest 
in someone's home.
```

**Section 3 — The Menu:**
```
Heading: A Table for Every Craving

Copy:
We didn't want to limit our guests to one cuisine. The Singh Saab menu 
spans Chinese starters and mains, hearty soups, and indulgent appetizers — 
because a great evening out should have something for everyone at the table.

Every dish, from our signature Singh Saab Spl. Soup to our Chicken 65, 
is made with the same intent that shaped this space: thoughtful, bold, 
and built to be remembered.
```

**Section 4 — The Experience:**
```
Heading: More Than a Meal

Copy:
Our guests tell us this themselves — The Singh Saab has become the place 
for birthdays, anniversaries, and the evenings that matter. With 4.6 stars 
and over a thousand reviews, we know we're doing something right.

But we're not done. Every visit, every detail, every dish — we're still 
building this, with intent, for you.
```
